## Component Details

### Source Cards
- Each source: name, work title, website, social handle
- Tappable links open in Safari
- Potential future: tip jar / support links

### Further Reading Links
- Curated links from Janjigian's handout
- Open in Safari (or in-app browser)
- Each shows title only; URL opens on tap

### Equipment List
- Simple list of recommended equipment
- From Janjigian's prep list
- No affiliate links (unless user wants to add later)

### Glossary
- Expandable cards (like PourCraft's TipCard)
- Alphabetically sorted
- ▶ collapsed state: shows term name only
- ▼ expanded state: shows term + definition
- Chevron rotates on expand/collapse (spring animation)
- Terms sourced from Janjigian's glossary
