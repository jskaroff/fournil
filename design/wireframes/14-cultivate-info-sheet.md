# Wireframe: Cultivate a New Starter Info Sheet

Full-screen informational guide for cultivating a starter from scratch. Presented
after creating a starter via the "Cultivate a new starter" onboarding path.

Based on the process described at wordloaf.org (tiny starter method).

## Content Structure

### Header
- 🌱 emoji, title "Growing a Starter from Scratch"
- Brief intro paragraph setting expectations (7-14 days)

### What You'll Need
- Simple bulleted list of materials
- Emphasis on whole grain flour and filtered water

### The Process (Day-by-Day)
- Step cards with emoji icons, organized by day ranges
- Each card has clear, concise instructions
- Visual progression from Day 1 through Day 14

### Tips
- Bulleted practical advice
- Link to Andrew Janjigian's detailed guide (outbound link, may include affiliate)

### "Log Your First Feed" CTA
- Large button at the bottom, sticky or prominently visible
- Navigates to **Log a Feed** (12-log-a-feed.md) for the newly created starter
- Subtitle: "When your starter is ready, log your first feeding to start tracking its health."

---

## Component Details

### Step Cards
- Rounded cards with `flourDust` background
- Emoji icon at top-left
- Clear, scannable text
- Consistent spacing between cards

### Layout
- Scrollable single column
- Max content width ~700px, centered on landscape iPad
- CTA button is at the bottom of the scroll, or sticky at bottom of screen

### Link to Source
- "Not-Quite-So-Tiny Starter" link opens in Safari
- May include affiliate link (respects affiliate toggle in Settings)

---

## Interactions

- **Scroll** through the guide at own pace
- **"Log Your First Feed"** → navigates to the feeding form for this starter
- **Back button** → returns to Starter Details (starter has been created but has no feedings)
- **External link** → opens in Safari
