Alternate Design Directions

Three distinct aesthetic directions for Fournil, each covering all four main screens: Bake Tab, Starter Tab, Bread Log, and Bake Tracker.

Alt 01
Midnight Craftsman

Dark forge aesthetic. Amber glows against deep charcoal. The baker who rises at 4am. Intimate, focused, alive with warmth in the darkness.

Swiss Precision

Field Journal
